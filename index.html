About

Academic profile

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), Bangladesh. My work focuses on developing intelligent systems that address practical challenges in healthcare, safety, and infrastructure monitoring.

My undergraduate thesis explored skin lesion image segmentation using transfer learning. My current MSc research investigates a multi-modal deep learning framework for road damage detection. Across my research projects, I have worked with deep learning, computer vision, medical image analysis, machine learning, and explainable artificial intelligence.
